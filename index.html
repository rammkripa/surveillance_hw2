Crossing Borders: Lena's (Not-So) Hidden Journey

In today's world, wristbands are more than just a fashion statement. They're exceptionally complex tiny computers tasked with recording our health data for monitoring our well-being and fitness levels. However, these cool features come at the cost of privacy. With enterprises, brokers, and even governments willing to pay a pretty penny for the data our wristbands collect, individuals are increasingly vulnerable to surveillance and the potential misuse of their personal information.

This map shows the journey of an 18-year-old woman.
